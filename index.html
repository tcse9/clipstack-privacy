Privacy Policy

App: ClipStack  ·  Effective: April 24, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 24, 2026

Short version: ClipStack does not collect, read, or transmit your clipboard content. Everything you copy stays on your device (and optionally in your private iCloud). We use RevenueCat solely to manage your subscription — it never sees your clips.

1. Who We Are

ClipStack is an independently developed iOS app. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy mean the individual developer reachable at taufiq.dev9@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect as little as possible. Here is the full picture:

Data Where it goes Who can see it
Clipboard content (your clips) Stored locally on your device via SwiftData. Optionally synced to your private iCloud (CloudKit) if you enable iCloud on your Apple ID. Only you. We have no access.
Tags, expiry dates, pin status Same as above — local device / your private iCloud. Only you.
Subscription status Managed by RevenueCat. An anonymous identifier (not linked to your name or email) is used to track whether you have an active Pro subscription. Us and RevenueCat (see Section 4).
Crash and diagnostic logs If you opt in to sharing diagnostics with developers in iOS Settings, Apple may share anonymised crash reports with us. This data contains no clipboard content. Us, via Apple's developer tools. Never your clips.

We do not collect: your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or any other data beyond what is listed above.

3. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, ClipStack uses Apple's CloudKit private database to sync your clips across your own devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account under your Apple ID — we have no technical ability to read it. Apple's iCloud security and encryption policies apply. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings → your Apple ID → iCloud → ClipStack.

4. RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

ClipStack uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. When you purchase or restore a Pro subscription, RevenueCat receives:

  • An anonymous app user ID generated by RevenueCat (not your Apple ID or email).
  • Purchase receipt information from Apple to verify your subscription status.
  • Anonymised usage events (e.g., subscription started, renewed, cancelled).

RevenueCat does not receive any of your clipboard content, tags, or personal data beyond the above. All payment processing is handled by Apple — RevenueCat only receives receipt data that Apple provides. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

5. Apple Intelligence & On-Device AI

ClipStack's Smart Clip features (auto-categorisation, summaries, "Ask Your Clips" search, and translation) use the FoundationModels framework on iOS 26+. All AI processing runs entirely on your device — your clipboard content is never sent to any server for these features. These features require a device that supports Apple Intelligence and will not activate on unsupported devices.

6. Clipboard Access

ClipStack reads your device clipboard only when:

  • You are actively using the app and iOS detects new content on the clipboard, prompting you to save it.
  • You explicitly tap to save a clip from the Share Sheet extension.
  • You use the custom keyboard extension to paste a saved clip.

On iOS 16+, the system shows a banner notification whenever an app reads the clipboard. ClipStack never reads the clipboard silently in the background.

7. App Extensions

ClipStack includes a Share Sheet extension, a custom keyboard extension, and a Home Screen widget. These extensions share access to your locally stored clips via a private iOS App Group container — the data does not leave your device through this mechanism. The same privacy guarantees apply to the extensions as to the main app.

8. Children's Privacy

ClipStack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Your clips are stored until you delete them within the app, or until you delete the app from your device. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can delete iCloud data from iOS Settings → your Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage → ClipStack. RevenueCat anonymised purchase data is retained per their own data retention policy.

10. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data beyond an anonymous subscription identifier, there is typically nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on our end. If you have questions about your RevenueCat data, contact RevenueCat directly. If you have any other privacy questions or requests, email us at taufiq.dev9@gmail.com.

11. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service ClipStack integrates with is RevenueCat (see Section 4). We do not integrate advertising networks, analytics SDKs, social SDKs, or tracking frameworks of any kind.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If the changes are material, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance.

13. Contact

Privacy questions or concerns? Email taufiq.dev9@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
