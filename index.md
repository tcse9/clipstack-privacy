# Privacy Policy

**ClipStack — Clipboard Manager**

*Last updated: March 23, 2026*

## Overview

ClipStack is a clipboard manager app for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data ClipStack collects, how it is used, and your rights.

**The short version: ClipStack does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to external servers. Everything stays on your device.**

## Data Collection

ClipStack does **not** collect:

- Personal information (name, email, address)
- Analytics or usage data
- Crash reports
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Advertising data

## Data Storage

All clipboard data you save in ClipStack is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not uploaded to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Is not accessible to the developer

If you delete the app, all saved clipboard data is permanently removed from your device.

## Clipboard Access

ClipStack reads your device clipboard **only when the app is open and in the foreground**. iOS displays a system notification ("ClipStack pasted from...") each time the clipboard is accessed. ClipStack does not read your clipboard in the background.

## In-App Purchases

ClipStack offers a one-time in-app purchase ("ClipStack Pro") processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions.

## Third-Party Services

ClipStack does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

ClipStack does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since the app collects no data from any user, it is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
